Teachers:

Description:

On this page, you will find the materials I used for my contribution to David Hajage's course on Méthodes avancées d'analyse en pharmacoépidémiologie. During this course, I conducted a 3-hour lecture and a 2-hour lab session focused on introducing causal Directed Acyclic Graphs (DAGs) and the principles of causal effect identification.
The only slides available on this page are those specifically used in my sessions of the course.
If you have any questions or need additional information about these topics, feel free to reach out!

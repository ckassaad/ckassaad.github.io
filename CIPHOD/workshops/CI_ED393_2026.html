Workshop on causal inference

In collaboration with the doctoral school 393, the CIPHOD, the CLEPIVIR and the PEPITES teams of IPLESP are co-organizing a workshop on causal inference, which will be held from June 8th to June 12th in room 207 at Sorbonne University - Faculty of Health – Saint-Antoine Hospital. The number of participants is limited to 20 doctoral students from ED393 and 20 members of IPLESP.

This workshop aims to introduce the basic concepts of causal inference.

Schedule

June 8 9h-10h30 and 11h-12h Causal reasoning
June 8 14h-15h30 and 16h-17h Counterfactual reasoning
June 9 9h-10h30 and 11h-12h Causal estimation and Emulated trials
June 9 14h-15h30 and 16h-17h TMLE
June 10 9h-10h30 and 11h-12h Applications
June 10 15h-17h On hands applications
June 11 9h-10h30 and 11h-12h Causal Discovery
June 11 14h-15h30 and 16h-17h TabPFN
June 12 10h-17h On hands applications

In collaboration with the doctoral school 393, the CIPHOD, the CLEPIVIR and the PEPITES teams of IPLESP are co-organizing a workshop on causal inference, which will be held on June 2, 3, and 4 in room 210 at Sorbonne University - Faculty of Health â€“ Saint-Antoine Hospital.

One Day Hackathons

In this monthly hackathon event, we aim to temporarily set aside our usual tasks and come together as a team to focus intensively on a single subject that will be defined by other teams in the iPLesp institute. This collaborative effort allows us to leverage diverse perspectives, share expertise, and push the boundaries of our understanding in causal inference and epidemiology. Additionally, if you'd like to contribute to defining the subjects, we encourage you to participate in our separate monthly causal meetings dedicated to this purpose.

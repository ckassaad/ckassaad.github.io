Causality reading group

In this reading group we aim delve into the latest research and methodologies about causal inference while examining their relevance to epidemiology through dynamic discussions. Meetings will be held every month, with each session featuring a selected paper or chapter. A designated discussant will guide the session, fostering critical analysis and stimulating engaging conversations. All members of iPLesp are invited to join us! Stay tuned for meeting announcements and selected readings.

For inquiries or to express interest in leading a session or suggesting topics, feel free to reach out to us.

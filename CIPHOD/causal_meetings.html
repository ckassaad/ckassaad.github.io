Causal meetings

This monthly meeting is dedicated to to facilitate open discussions regarding the practical applications of causal inference in epidemiology, as well as to explore potential collaborations between the CIPHOD team and other teams within iPLesp. Participants are encouraged to share their insights, experiences, and ideas regarding epidemiological topics where causal inference can be useful.

Additionally, these meetings provide an opportunity to define the subjects for our monthly hackathon events. By gathering input from all participants, we ensure that the chosen subjects align with the interests of all teams within iPLesp.

We invite all members all teams within iPLesp to join us for these monthly meetings. Your participation and contributions are invaluable as we work together to advance causal inference research and its applications in epidemiology.
